## Core Vision

**DCP turns domains into a revolutionary programmable primitive** — combining extreme speed, deep programmability, strong safety, and reduced operational liability.

We want developers to experience domain-level changes as **instant, safe, and programmable** in ways that current tools do not deliver.

---

## What Makes DCP Revolutionary

### 1. Revolutionary Speed
- Sub-200ms p99 routing activation for most changes (DNS-free hot path).
- True instant, atomic rollbacks.
- Highly predictable tail latency at global scale.
- Predictive pre-warming and hardware-aware optimizations.

### 2. Revolutionary Domain Programmability
- Domains as first-class programmable units (routing + compute + state integrated and versioned together).
- Intent-based model with safe compilation.
- Built-in versioning, provenance, and reversibility as core features.
- Programmable Edge and Domain State Primitives feel native to the domain model.

### 3. Speed + Safety Without the Usual Trade-offs
- High velocity without sacrificing safety or auditability.
- AI assistance that accelerates development while respecting strong policy boundaries.

### 4. Performance with Reduced Liability
- World-class speed by running on Cloudflare’s infrastructure (Pingora + Workers + Durable Objects).
- DCP owns the intelligent control plane (compilation, safety, orchestration) while shifting most runtime liability to proven global infrastructure.

---

## Differentiation

- Not just "domains on Workers" — a full control plane that makes domains programmable with safety and speed as first-class concerns.
- Better integration between routing, compute, and state than current edge platforms.
- Stronger emphasis on instant reversibility and provenance.
